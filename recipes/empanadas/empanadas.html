Empanadas

The empanada is more than just a snack; it is a symbol of Venezuelan culture and tradition. These little pockets of joy come in different varieties, each with its own unique flavor and filling. From meat to cheese to plantains, the empanada has something for everyone. But more than its delicious taste, the empanada represents the warmth and hospitality of the Venezuelan people. It is a food that brings families together, a comfort during hard times, and a celebration during happy moments.
