Empanadas

Empanadas is the heart of venezuelans breakfast

Original version of the Venezulean recipe for empanadas. Rich and delicious. Any meat can be used for this recipe, just be sure to cut it up into tiny pieces.

  • Corn Flour
  • Meat
  • a cup of water
  • salt
  • oil
  1. Mix the flour with the water and the salt until you have a good consistency
  2. expand a portion of the dough in a circular shape
  3. add the meat and close the mass creating a semi-moon shape
  4. deep fry each side for 2 minutes in a
  5. enjoy!
