Arepa

-----The heart of Venezuela,The iconic Arepa------

The arepa is more than just a corn cake; it is the heart and soul of Venezuela. This iconic dish represents the country's rich cultural heritage and symbolizes the resilience and creativity of its people. The arepa is a staple food that has been enjoyed by generations, from humble street vendors to high-end restaurants. It is a versatile dish that can be filled with anything from cheese and ham to black beans and plantains. The arepa is a true reflection of the diversity and vibrancy of Venezuela.

Ingredients

  • Shredded chicken
  • Avocado
  • Mayonnaise
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken: Boil or bake the chicken until fully cooked, then shred it into small pieces.
  2. Prepare the avocado mixture: In a bowl, mash the avocado with a fork until it's creamy but still slightly chunky. Add chopped onion, cilantro, and lemon juice if desired.
  3. Mix the chicken and avocado mixture: Add the shredded chicken to the bowl with the avocado mixture, then stir until well combined.
  4. Add the mayonnaise: Add mayonnaise to the bowl and mix everything together until the filling is creamy and evenly coated
  5. Season to taste: Add salt and pepper to taste, and adjust any other seasonings as needed.
  6. Serve: Spoon the Reina Pepiada filling onto an arepa, and enjoy!
